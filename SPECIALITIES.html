UMCSpecialities

At UNITED Medical Center, we offer you comprehensive treatment services that are specifically tailored to your needs. Our team of highly rated doctors are from a long list of specialties and super-specialties and are very experienced in their own fields. Each department here is fully-equipped with the latest devices to further assist our brilliant doctors in performing the latest diagnostic and therapeutic procedures practised in their fields of specialisation.

ANAESTHESIOLOGY

The department is fully-equipped with anaesthesia machines, monitors, ventilators, safety gadgets and Continuous drug delivery systems.
These machines are operated expertly by highly-skilled and experienced anaesthesiologists.

CARDIOLOGY

The Department of Cardiology here has emerged as a symbol of trust and offers comprehensive and holistic approaches towards heart care ranging from preventive to early detection and emergency treatments.

DENTISTRY

the dentistry department is fully-equipped with ultra modern equipments and facilities for orthodontic procedures, cosmetic modifications and oral surgical procedures. Wherever possible, there is emphasis on conservative treatment.

DERMATOLOGY

The department is fully equipped with the latest technologies including CO2 Laser, Radio frequency, Electrocautry, PRP kit, Centrifuge machine, Derma Roller.

DIETETICS

United Medical Centres is a leading healthcare provider where all medical disciplines come together under one roof. The Department of Dietetics is one such specialty.

EMERGENCY MEDICINE

UMC is well-equipped with relevant resuscitation devices including fully-equipped crash carts, fully-equipped ambulance services, portable ventilators, portable x-rays, cardiac and BP monitors, Pulse oximeters, ECG, triage room, minor procedure room.

ENDOCRINOLOGY

Our specialists here are on a mission to control the menace of these hormone-related disorders in an effective and efficient manner. The doctors are supported by state-of-the-art diagnostic tools.
